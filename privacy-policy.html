1. Introduction

Garment Me ("the App") is an AI-powered virtual try-on application for Android devices. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

2. App Permissions

Required Access

  • Camera: For real-time virtual try-on experiences
  • Photo Library: To use existing images for outfit simulations
  • Storage: Optional saving of virtual try-on results

All permissions are used exclusively for core app functionality and can be revoked at any time through device settings.

3. Data Collection and Usage

  • Local Processing: Body measurements and facial features are processed temporarily for virtual try-ons
  • No Biometric Storage: All biometric data is processed in real-time and never stored
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous usage data (device type, session length) for app improvements
  • User Preferences: Clothing choices and size selections stored locally on your device

4. Security

  • On-Device Processing: 95% of AI calculations occur locally on your device
  • Encrypted Transfers: Any cloud processing uses TLS 1.2+ encryption
  • Automatic Deletion: All processed images are purged within 24 hours

5. Changes to Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect new features or regulations. Significant changes will be notified through app updates.
